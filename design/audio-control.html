AudioControl

One component, three variants (design system §3.2). Everything on this page is live: tap to hear the states. Simulated playback is 2s.

A · Inline speaker

Default → playing. Gold glyph swaps to animated gold bars only while audio is actually playing (the one sanctioned active animation). 44px target, push-down press.

A2 · Speaker + speed toggle

Speed pill. Tap cycles 1× → 0.75×. Slowed state: gold text + gold border. Resets to 1× on next item (per-item, never sticky). Used in listening exercises only.

C · Chunk pill (tap = hear the chunk)

la cuenta
The pill IS the trigger. No icon inside. Playing state: gold border + soft gold ring while its audio plays. On presentation cards and the correction sheet.

Disabled

40% opacity, no interaction. Only legitimate case: TTS unavailable/errored. Never hide the control (layout stability, §4).
Component rules (§3.2) — APPROVED 2026-07-15: all audio glyphs in --accent-2 gold, never navy/gray (brand element). One AudioControl per audible thing; never two speakers for the same audio. Tapping while playing restarts (no pause button; clips are 1–4s). Bars animate only during real playback and freeze under prefers-reduced-motion. Speed pill appears only where slow-listening is pedagogically relevant (listen-and-type, understand-the-reply); presentation cards and correction sheet get plain speakers. Autoplay only where specced (presentation card, correction sheet, listening prompts).
