Tripfluent living specimen
Companion to the design constitution. This page imports the live styles.css, so every specimen below renders exactly as the app does, in both themes. Law captions cite the constitution family and the tokens at work.
Color tokens
Color & light. Gold (--accent) is attention, not decoration; green appears only at the moment of confirmation; red only at genuine error. Dark is an atmosphere (--ground gradient + frosted --bg-card), never flat black. Every color change lands in BOTH dark blocks (prefers-color-scheme + [data-theme="dark"]).
Elevation & press physics
Elevation. Rest = --shadow (0 3px 0, a physical ledge). Press = translateY(2px) + --shadow-press (the ledge collapses: push-down, §2.1). Nothing floats with soft blur; everything sits. Hold the buttons above to feel it, live from styles.css.

Type. Display font (--font-display, Plus Jakarta Sans) for Spanish on stage and controls; text font (Inter) for supporting prose. Every tappable sets font-family explicitly: buttons do not inherit. Ligatures are off app-wide (no-common-ligatures): learners must see letters, not glyph fusions.
Controls
AudioControl (stand-in markup; the live component is built by soundIcon() in ui.js: one component, no ad-hoc speakers)
Controls. Choices are full-width cards on --bg-card with the shared ledge shadow; the speaker is a 44px circular target. All audio goes through AudioControl (glyph law: the speaker glyph belongs to one component).
urgent row + one-time arrival breath
Action surfaces. The row is a menu; prominence escalates in place: quiet → count badge (.pbadge, "8 due") → urgent field ("12 fading"). Urgency announces itself ONCE: .arrive plays breathFill/breathHalo twice (1300ms swells), then stillness. Never loops, never re-fires on re-render.
Tiles & keys (supply rule)
Supply rule. Live supply (word tiles, letter keys) is material: card background + ledge shadow, ready to be picked up. Spent supply recedes to opacity 0.28, flat, no shadow: it visibly leaves the economy. Letter keys are 46×48px minimum touch targets. Trays offer BASE letters only, never á and a side by side: the accent materializes at the green settle (tray ruling, 2026-07-23).
The blanks family
Me puede t-aer -a
filled · next (bright) · pending (dim)
Slot color law. Pending slots wait in neutral dim (2px underline, --text-dim); exactly ONE slot is bright: the next one (--accent, 3px). A filled slot confirms with a green sweep. A wrong tap shakes the tray and flashes the slot red; the slot never fills with the wrong letter. Word gaps are 0.3em flex gaps (the 2px artifact gap fused words: derivation find, 2026-07-23).
Dials
Dials. Track = --ring-track (translucent dark in light, near-opaque in dark: dials must read over the photo). Readiness carries the gold pace tick (Submariner index, outside the track: fill position vs tick = ahead/behind; no arc painting). Bands: red <40, gold 40–64, green ≥65. Dials move only when their number moves (ceremony contradiction rule).
Resolution anatomy
Can you bring me the bill

green wash = correct only, never on a graceful miss
Resolution vocabulary. The resolved card: es on stage (display 21/500), the en gloss as a 13px footnote (.res-en), one AudioControl row. The green wash (.qcorrect) marks a correct answer; a graceful miss (letter rungs, 2+ wrong taps) resolves quietly: no wash, no Stronger tick, no correction sheet, but the SRS records the miss.
